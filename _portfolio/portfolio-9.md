The system integrates multi-sensor perception, BEV-based scene representation, dynamic object tracking, and decision-making to handle complex mixed-traffic scenarios, including dense pedestrians, cyclists, and unstructured road interactions. It enables safe, smooth, and reliable autonomous driving in challenging city conditions, demonstrating production-ready performance for urban deployment.
